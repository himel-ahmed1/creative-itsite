About Chattogram Branch


Creative IT Institute always ensures to provide quality IT training all over Bangladesh.
This institute never compromises on solving the unemployment problem of Bangladesh.
For this reason, a branch in Chattogram was established on 1st October in 2018. Since
then, it has been running successfully. It is the leading IT institute in Chattogram city.

This branch is well decorated with upgraded tools for our students convenience. As a
result, our students of Chattogram are delighted receiving a quality training. Many of our
students magnificently achieved their success both in local and international
marketplaces.
